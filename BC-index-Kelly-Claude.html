📋 Calculation Methodology
✓ Coachees are people leaders with direct reports
✓ Coaching reduces leader turnover by [Industry Benchmark]% — This is based on your selected industry and research-backed coaching effectiveness studies. Different industries see different coaching impact (e.g., Hospitality: 25%, Banking: 20%, Tech: 16%)
✓ Spillover effect: coaching leaders reduces their team's turnover by 5% (fixed)
✓ Each leader has ~6 direct reports (teams impacted by coaching)
✓ Replacement cost = 100% of annual salary (hiring + onboarding + lost productivity)
✓ Direct report salary = 65% of leader's salary
✓ Profit improvement: 10 basis points per coachee on company profit margin (typically 6%)
ℹ️ About Industry Benchmarks: This calculator uses industry-specific benchmarks derived from 2024-2025 coaching effectiveness research. The turnover reduction percentage adjusts based on the industry you select in Step 1, ensuring the ROI calculation reflects the realistic impact for your specific sector.
