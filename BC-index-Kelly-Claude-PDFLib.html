📋 How This Is Calculated
✓ Leadership drives retention. Coached executives develop stronger communication and resilience — reducing their own likelihood of leaving and improving their team's stability too.
✓ Turnover is expensive. Replacing a senior leader costs ~100% of their annual salary (recruitment, onboarding, lost productivity). Every leader retained through coaching avoids that cost.
✓ Industry-calibrated impact. Turnover reduction is sector-specific, from 2024–2025 research. Examples: Hospitality 25%, Banking 20%, IT 16%, Public Sector 10%.
✓ Team spillover (5%). Each leader manages ~6 direct reports. Better leadership reduces team turnover by 5%, calculated at 65% of the leader's salary.
✓ Profit improvement. Stronger leadership lifts business performance, modelled as a 10 basis-point gain per coachee on company profit (6% assumed margin).
ℹ️ ROI = (Benefits − Investment) ÷ Investment. Benefits cover leader retention, team spillover, and profit improvement. All figures are directional estimates.
